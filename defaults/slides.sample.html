What is this?

  • A minimalistic html 5 slide app
  • play slides written in html
  • with some necessary effects, not fancy though
  • can support internal archors and jumps with browser history support

Keybindings

  • Space, right, down - next slide
  • Backspace, left, up - prev slide
  • o - enter presenting mode (the original window becomes the controller)

That's it. Enjoy!
